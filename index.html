You train directly with me. One coach, one plan, start to finish.

I grew up competing in Israel, won the national championship twice, and came to the United States to run Division I track. I hold a coaching certification from Tel Aviv University. I've been around the best coaches and athletes in the sport. This is what I do.

Before I write a single workout, I get to know you. Your race, your history, your schedule, how you've trained before. Then I build a plan around your season and adjust it every week based on how you're actually doing.

I coach 5k, 10k, track, half marathon, marathon, and high school runners. Whatever your goal is, I'll get you there.

Home

02

Plan

Your entire season mapped out, from the first base week to race day. Every phase, every week.

Stats

01 Dashboard

Open the app and your day is clear

Today's workout, your week's progress, and a note from your coach. No guessing, nothing to search for. You just run. That's how consistency stops being a decision.

02 Season Journey

You know where you're headed

Base, build, peak, race day. Every phase has a date and a purpose. When you see the whole season laid out, individual workouts make sense, even the hard ones and the easy ones.

03 Training Data

Everything you've done is tracked

Your pace over time, how far you've run, how hard you've pushed. Real numbers showing the real picture. Seeing progress is what keeps you going on the days you don't feel like it.

04 Workout Detail

Not just the distance. Everything.

The sets, the pace, the target, and a personal note from your coach. Everything you need for the workout, right there. A clear plan means no doubt, and no doubt means you actually execute.
