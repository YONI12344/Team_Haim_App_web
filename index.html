Your Coach

Every athlete in Team Haim trains with one coach, one plan, one standard. Not an anonymous list.

I've been in the running world my entire life. Two-time Israeli national champion. Competed as an NCAA Division I distance runner in the United States. I've trained alongside and learned from some of the best coaches and athletes in the world — and everything I learned, I'm here to give to you.

The App

Your entire training, one place

Four screens that separate athletes who break through from those who plateau.

01

Home

Today's workout, weekly progress, and a direct note from your coach — every time you open the app.

02

Plan

Your full season from base training through race day, with every phase and milestone mapped out.

03

Stats

Weekly kilometers, effort scores, average pace. Charts that reveal the real trend behind your training.

04

Coach Chat

A question about a set, a pain, a schedule change. A direct reply from your coach — not a bot.

01 — Dashboard

Every morning starts here

Open the app and you see today's workout, your week's progress, and a note from your coach. No guessing. No digging through emails or spreadsheets. You just run. That's how consistency stops being a choice and becomes automatic.

02 — Season Journey

Always know where you're headed

Base. Build. Peak. Race day. Every phase mapped with dates and weekly targets. When you can see the full picture, you trust each day's workout — even the hard ones, and especially the easy ones. Every phase exists for a reason.

03 — Training Data

When you see it, you keep going

Pace trends, weekly kilometers, effort load across months. Every workout you've finished lives here. Seeing your progress in real numbers is what keeps you moving on the days you don't feel like it. The data is honest. Use it.

04 — Workout Detail

Exactly what to do. Exactly how.

Not just "run 8km." Every workout includes sets, reps, target pace, specific instructions, and personal notes from Coach Yoni. A clear plan eliminates doubt — and doubt is where training falls apart.
