Your Coach

Yehonatan (Yoni) Haim

Every athlete in Team Haim trains with one coach, one plan, one standard — not an anonymous list.

I've been in the running world my entire life. Two-time Israeli national champion. Competed as an NCAA Division I distance runner in the United States. I've trained alongside the best coaches and athletes in the world — and everything I learned, I'm here to give to you.

Team Haim runs on one principle: runners improve fastest when a coach truly knows them — their event, their history, and the exact week they're in. Every athlete goes through a personal intake call before a single workout is written. Then your plan is built around your season phases: base, build, peak — updated based on how you respond.

Direct communication with your coach, Strava integration, and data that shows exactly where you stand. Everything in one place.

The App

Your entire training, one place

Four screens that separate athletes who break through from those who plateau.

01

Home

Today's workout, weekly progress, and a direct note from your coach every time you open the app.

02

Plan

Your full season from base training through race day — every phase and milestone mapped out.

03

Stats

Weekly kilometers, effort scores, average pace. Charts that reveal the real trend in your training.

04

Coach Chat

A question about a set, a pain, a schedule change. A direct reply from your coach — not a bot.

01 — Dashboard

Every morning starts here

Open the app and see today's workout, your week's progress, and a note from your coach. No guessing, no digging through emails. You just run. That's how consistency becomes automatic.

02 — Season Journey

Always know where you're headed

Base. Build. Peak. Race day. Every phase mapped with dates and weekly targets. When you see the full picture, you trust each day's workout — even the hard ones, and especially the easy ones.

03 — Training Data

When you see it, you keep going

Pace trends, weekly kilometers, effort load across months. Every workout you've finished is tracked here. Seeing your progress in real numbers is what keeps you moving on the days you don't feel like it.

04 — Workout Detail

Exactly what to do. Exactly how.

Not just "run 8km." Every workout includes sets, reps, target pace, and personal notes from Coach Yoni. A clear plan eliminates doubt — and doubt is where training falls apart.
